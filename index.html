Basic Gameplay Overview

2 - 4 players take on the role of Survivors trying to escape from the trial initiated by an unknown force called the Entity.

Another player takes on the role of the Killer who hunts the Survivors and attempts to sacrifice them to the Entity.

If the Survivors can escape the trial by opening the exit gate before the Killer completes the sacrifice progress, they win the game.

In a group with less than 5 players, some may control multiple Survivors.
