Survivor Actions

1. Search an Occupied Locker

Survivor rolls a Skill Check. Failure: picked up by Killer; success: locker removed.

2. Scout with Crow Prop

Remove Crow Prop and gain 1 Blood Point.

3. Damage a Generator

Remove all progress tokens.

4. Venerate Totem

Gain 2 Blood Points.

5. Attack a Survivor

Place a Wounded Ring below the figure.

6. Pick Up

Can only pick up a Survivor not wounded this round. If an unoccupied hook is in the space, the Survivor is sacrificed, and a progress token is added. If not, give the survivor 1-4 dice then carry the Survivor to another space corresponding to the dice
[Example : give the survivor 2 dices mean can move 2 spaces]
the survivor roll 1–4 dice. Survivor rolls a 5 (Great Success): escapes. Any failure: Killer gains 1 Blood Point. Sacrificed Survivors skip their turn.
