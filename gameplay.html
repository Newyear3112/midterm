Gameplay

Gameplay occurs in rounds. Each round is divided into four phases: 1. Planning, 2. Survivors Act, 3. Killer Act, and 4. Clean Up.

Planning

During the Planning phase, each player selects and places one movement card face down in front of them. The Killer selects two movement cards and places them in a face-down row. Survivors may communicate, but all messages must be public and audible to the Killer.

Survivors Act

In turn order, starting with the Survivor holding the First Player marker, each Survivor takes their turn. The active player first reveals their movement card and moves their figure along a connected path matching the color of that path. Note: Yellow vault paths are one-way only.

If any props in the new space are face down, the Survivor may flip one face up. They then interact with one revealed prop in the space. All actions happen in this order: MOVE, then INTERACT.

If the revealed card has no legal moves, the player cannot interact with the prop that turn. Once all Survivors have completed their turns, the Killer acts.

Survivor Actions

Killer Act

The Killer follows a move → interact sequence. They start with their left movement card, then the right. The same rules that apply to Survivors also apply to the Killer. The Wait card allows the Killer to remain in the same space and interact with a prop. The Killer may use their special power once per round instead of interacting.

At the end of their second turn, the Killer may spend four Blood Points to set a third movement card and take another turn. The Killer may only take one bonus turn per round.

Killer Actions

Clean Up

Once the Killer’s turn is complete, the Clean Up phase begins:

  • For each sacrificed Survivor, place a sacrifice token on the Sacrifice Progress Bar.
  • Remove the Entity token from the board.
  • Pass the First Player marker clockwise to the next Survivor.
  • All players return their movement cards to their hands and start a new round.
  • If Survivors open the exit gate before the Killer reaches eight sacrifice tokens, they win the game.
